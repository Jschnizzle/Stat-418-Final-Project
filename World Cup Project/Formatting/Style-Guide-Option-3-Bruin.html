Bruin

Unmistakably UCLA — bright blue and bold gold, set in a clean geometric sans. Goldman's sky blue stays on as a quiet supporting tone for data.

Blend & intent

Roughly 70% UCLA, 30% Goldman Sachs.

Bruin leads with school spirit. UCLA blue and a confident gold do most of the talking, set in Archivo — a geometric grotesque that stands in for UCLA's Karbon. Goldman shows up subtly: the muted sky blue as a secondary data color and the same disciplined, white-forward restraint that keeps the brightness from tipping into noise. Best if you want the work to read as proudly UCLA first.

A four-blue ramp (Dark → UCLA → Sky → Light) gives you ordered, accessible chart series; gold is the standout marker. Keep gold for fills and rules only — never body text, which fails contrast on white.

Typography

One geometric sans across headlines and body — the modern, clean UCLA voice — with a monospace for R code.

Body — Archivo (Regular)

Archivo is an open geometric grotesque that closely tracks UCLA's Karbon: clean, contemporary, and timeless. Using one family at multiple weights keeps the system simple and unmistakably modern across a PDF, a script, and a slide deck.
