Westwood

A balanced identity — UCLA blue and gold meet Goldman's sky blue and serif poise in equal measure. Collegiate confidence with a professional finish.

Blend & intent

Roughly 50% UCLA, 50% Goldman Sachs.

Westwood splits the difference. UCLA blue leads and gold is a genuine accent (not just a hairline), giving the work clear school identity. Goldman contributes the serif headline voice, the calmer sky blue as a secondary, and a disciplined, white-forward layout. The result reads as a sharp academic deliverable that still looks like it came from a firm.

Use the two blues to separate data series in charts; reserve gold for the single most important mark or call-to-attention. Set body text in Charcoal, not pure black, for a softer institutional feel.

Typography

Serif headlines with a geometric sans body — the balance point between Goldman's serif tradition and UCLA's clean, modern sans.

Display / Headings — Source Serif 4

Acquire, understand, model, communicate.

Body — Inter

Inter is a contemporary, highly legible sans that echoes UCLA's Helvetica/Karbon lineage while staying free and reproducible. Paired with a serif headline, it keeps reports readable and slides crisp without feeling either stuffy or sporty.

Monospace / Code — JetBrains Mono
